# Overview

## Innovative Technologies

Saab is at the cutting edge of innovation, developing advanced systems for aerospace, land, and sea applications. Its products range from state-of-the-art fighter jets and radar systems to sophisticated submarine technology.

## Commitment to Global Security

With a focus on enhancing global safety, Saab’s solutions are designed to meet the most demanding security challenges. The company works closely with governments and organizations worldwide to deliver tailored solutions that ensure long-term stability.

## Why We Invest in Saab

Our investment in Saab reflects our confidence in its ability to combine technical excellence with strong operational performance. Saab’s dedication to innovation and security aligns perfectly with our strategy of supporting companies that contribute to long-term global growth and resilience.
